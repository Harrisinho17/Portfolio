Fifa Case Study

This project focused on analyzing year-to-date (YTD) data retrieved from FIFA’s Interactive Voice Response (IVR) system with the goal of identifying areas for optimization. The case study required me to clean, analyze, and visualize the data within a strict four-day timeframe, pushing me to effectively manage my time and apply my technical skills under pressure. As my first professional case study for an interview, this project was a rewarding challenge that allowed me to demonstrate my abilities while also identifying areas for growth.

Although the interview process is still ongoing and I cannot share my findings or dashboard publicly yet, I plan to publish them once permitted. This experience was a valuable challenge that showcased my abilities while highlighting areas for personal growth.
