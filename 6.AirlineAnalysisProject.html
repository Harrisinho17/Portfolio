Airline Analysis Project

Overview

This project analyzes datasets from the MIT Airline Data website MIT Airline Data website to uncover trends in ticket prices, the impact of fuel costs, flight delay factors, route profitability, and passenger satisfaction issues. It aims to provide actionable insights for airlines and stakeholders through data cleaning, analysis, modeling, and visualization, enabling informed decisions to enhance efficiency, profitability, and customer experience.
