Airline Analysis Project

Overview

Using things I have learned from my previous projects, I plan to conduct Data Journalism to discover practical strategies that airlines can use to enhance customer reviews and maintain profitability. My approach will include Price Modeling to optimize ticket pricing, Density Metrics to assess flight capacities, and AI and Machine Learning to analyze customer sentiment from reviews. I will also use Web-Scraping and Data Analysis to gather and examine relevant data. This comprehensive method will help airlines make informed decisions to improve both customer satisfaction and business performance.
