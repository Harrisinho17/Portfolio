Airline Analysis Project

Overview

Using the insights I’ve gained from previous projects, I plan to conduct Data Journalism to delve into several key questions about airlines that I have flown with in the past. With a strong foundation in ethical data scraping, data visualization, and analysis, I aim to explore trends that could significantly influence the future of the airline sector. These include evaluating the impact of ticket pricing strategies, understanding passenger sentiment, and assessing the potential for expanding route offerings. The airlines I have chosen for this analysis are American Airlines, Qatar Airways, Jetstar, Fiji Airways, and Qantas Airways.

Github Repository

Questions

What are the trends in airline ticket prices over the past decade?

How do fuel prices impact airline operating costs and ticket prices?

What factors contribute to flight delays, and which airlines are most frequently affected?

Which routes are the most and least profitable for major airlines?

What are the main causes for low passenger reviews and what can be done to resolve them?
