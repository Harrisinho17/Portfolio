Introduction

I’m Alexander Harris, a Help Desk Analyst and aspiring Data Scientist with a passion for leveraging technology to solve complex problems and drive data-driven decision-making. Based in Brisbane, QLD, I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from Florida International University and a certification in Google Data Analytics, equipping me with a solid foundation in analytical methodologies and technical acumen.

In my current role at ONE Sotheby’s International Realty, I’ve successfully led technology initiatives such as the deployment of an SSO application (OKTA), streamlining workflows for over 1,600 agents, and administering M365 licenses to a large, diverse workforce. My expertise extends to Python, SQL, Tableau, and process optimization, where I excel in transforming data into actionable insights to enhance productivity and efficiency. My fluency in English, alongside conversational Spanish and Portuguese, enables me to communicate effectively with diverse teams and clients, fostering collaboration and success.

I am deeply committed to continuous learning, as seen in my projects exploring Airbnb market trends in Brisbane and analyzing global COVID-19 data using SQL and Tableau. My goal is to contribute to impactful projects that harness the power of data and technology to drive meaningful results. Let’s connect to explore how my skills and experiences align with your needs!
