Introduction

I’m Alexander Harris, a Help Desk Analyst and aspiring Data Scientist with a passion for leveraging technology to solve complex problems and drive data-driven decision-making. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from Florida International University and a certification in Google Data Analytics, equipping me with a solid foundation in analytical methodologies and technical acumen.

In my current role at ONE Sotheby’s International Realty, I’ve been involved in several impactful technical initiatives aimed at enhancing IT operations and supporting the company’s growing real estate business. This includes optimizing user management processes, streamlining software license administration, and improving asset tracking through the implementation of modern IT tools. These efforts have contributed to a more efficient and reliable technological environment that supports both corporate staff and agents in the field. Motivated by curiosity and a passion for continuous improvement, I identified limitations in our ticketing system’s reporting capabilities and took the initiative to enhance them. By automating data workflows, I developed a custom dashboard that provided deeper insights into service requests and operational performance—delivering valuable business intelligence without the need for costly platform upgrades. My proficiency in Python, SQL, Excel, and Tableau has been crucial in driving these data-driven solutions, enabling more informed decision-making and streamlined IT processes.

I am deeply committed to continuous learning, as seen in my varioous projects. My goal is to contribute to impactful projects that harness the power of data and technology to drive meaningful results and further my understanding of the power of data. Let’s connect to explore how my skills and experiences align with your needs!
