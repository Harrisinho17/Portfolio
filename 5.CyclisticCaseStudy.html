Cyclistic Case Study Project

This project focuses on analyzing historical data from a fictional bike-sharing company to uncover key insights and trends. The primary objective is to explore patterns in user behavior, identify factors affecting member retention, and develop strategies to convert casual users into long-term subscribers. Through data cleaning, analysis, and visualization, actionable recommendations are made to enhance the company’s overall performance and customer engagement. This project was recomened to complete after completing the Google Data Analytics Certificate, however I will not be following the guide and I will produce fresh insights and analysis that I feel are important.

Cyclistic Case Study The first dashboard I created was based on the overall usage patterns. Below will be some interesting findings and I will come up with a business plan to convert casual users to members.

- On average, members used the Cyclistic service for a shorter duration (AVG Ride Minutes By User) However total uses by members were nearly double (Total Uses By User). This shows that members were more likely to utilize the service after becoming members. The three services Cyclistic is currently offering is Classic Bikes, Electric Bikes, and Electric Scooters with Electric Bikes being the most utilized. The majority of classic and electric bikes were used by members whereas the electric scooter with a much smaller sample size seems to have been used by casuals more.
